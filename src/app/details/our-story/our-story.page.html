About Boxeh

It's all about making it easier for people to cook & enjoy great meals at home.

We're a dedicated team of foodies, culinary artists, and creatives that work together as a family to bring zest and delight back to your kitchen, one Boxeh at a time.

We aim to provide households and families of different sizes and lifestyles with the opportunity to enjoy delicious, home-made meals without the hassle of meal planning and shopping.

We collaborate with top local chefs and trusted suppliers to thoughtfully plan, source, pack and deliver everything you need to make scrumptious meals at home to your front door.

Our founder Sahar was pursuing her MBA in the United Kingdom and was hooked on meal kit delivery services that allowed her to create wholesome meals at home while saving hours on grocery shopping and meal prep. That's when she found herself falling in love with cooking in a whole new way.

Upon her return to Amman, Sahar's desire to share her newfound passion for hassle-free cooking inspired her to build Boxeh to enable people to cook great meals at home without all the extra work.
